About the Developer

Name: Kelvin Banda

Born: 15 March, Chipata

Education: Computer Science Graduate

Role: Co-Founder, Nyota Tech

Kelvin Banda is a passionate software developer with a deep love for building apps and exploring new technologies. Born and raised in Chipata, he pursued a degree in Computer Science and went on to co-found Nyota Tech, a company dedicated to creating innovative digital solutions.

With a strong background in software engineering, Kelvin has a knack for turning ideas into functional, user-friendly applications. His passion for tech drives him to continuously learn, build, and contribute to the ever-evolving world of software development.

Groove Lab is one of his projects — a music learning and practice platform designed to help drummers and music enthusiasts improve their skills through interactive tools and lessons.
